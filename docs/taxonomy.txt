Context:
  - University
  - Lecturers hold Lectures
  - Students attend Lectures

Survey:
  - a Survey is conducted during Lectures
  - they can be started manually/automatically(scheduled)
  - Students answer Surveys via online interfaces which become available when a Survey is started
  - the Questions and Answer Options are based on Questionnaires

Questionnaire:
  - the Form of one or more Surveys
  - consists of Questions and Answer Options for Questions

User:
  - an online user

Physical Conditions:
  - physical conditions of a room in which a Lecture takes place
  - example: temperature, light/brightness, oxygen percentage
